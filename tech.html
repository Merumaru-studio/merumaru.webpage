  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 7 5800X

This machine is mainly used for hosting our server, running NAS storage, and occasionally as a CPU render node. It may also support video editing tasks in post-production later. Urgent upgrades needed: NVIDIA GPU (required for Arnold renderer compatibility) and significantly more RAM.

Planned Infrastructure Improvements

In the near future we intend to add a UGREEN NAS system to improve file accessibility, centralized storage and real-time collaboration when working together.

Production Tracking

We are still deciding on the best tracking solution. Autodesk Flow Production Tracking (formerly Shotgun) is our preferred choice due to its feature set, but the price is quite high. Most affordable alternatives currently lack important functionality we would need.

Strict No-AI Policy

We have a strict policy against using AI in any part of our production pipeline.
We believe AI destroys creativity rather than enhancing it.
We are not building rockets or conducting scientific research — and we actually learned grammar in school.

We do not want AI. We will not use AI. Ever.
That is a promise.
Everything you see from us is 100% human-made.

Why This Setup?

Our current hardware was originally purchased for studying purposes — it was never intended to produce short films or animated projects at a professional level.
Truly professional workstations and render nodes are prohibitively expensive for us at this stage.
Therefore we are deliberately working with what we already own and continuously optimizing the software environment.
As one important stability improvement we are currently migrating our main machines to Rocky Linux.
